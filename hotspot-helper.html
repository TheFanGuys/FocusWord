🗺️ Hotspot Helper

Find the percentage coordinates for clickable areas on a learning map, then paste them to Claude.

  1. Put your map image's path or URL below and tap Load.
  2. Tap the top-left corner of an object, then the bottom-right corner. A box appears.
  3. Type a label + chapters when asked.
  4. Repeat for each object, then tap Copy and send the box to Claude.
